Who I am

I am a Senior Resident in Emergency Medicine at SGPGIMS, Lucknow, and an active ICU physician at BMC Superspeciality Hospital. My daily work centres on the sickest patients — multi-organ dysfunction, septic shock, ARDS, and complex post-surgical care.

I created CritCare.in to share evidence-based critical care knowledge and to build practical tools that help physicians make better decisions at the bedside — especially in the smaller and resource-limited hospitals that form the backbone of Indian healthcare.
